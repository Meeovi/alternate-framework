## AlternateCMS Official CRM Plugin

This official Customer Relationship Managment (CRM) plugin is a powerful and fully featured pluggable app that extends the primary functionality of AlternateCMS to support a wide range of customers.

### Features include:

- Create and send emails to your customers with built in Email functionality.
- Create contracts for your customers with connections to projects, products, and other features of AlternateCMS.
- Deep Dives are great way to extend the built-in Gemba Board functionality. By diving deeper into issues, your team and others can collect information and collaborate easier.

- Targets are potential customers that are targeted for certain resources, projects, tickets, products, etc.
- Opportunities are great ways to engage with your team, customers, and partners across multiple channels.
- Leads are potential customers who have reached each milestone to become a valid customer. 
- Creating templates for emails, PDFs, documents, etc are a great way to customize your customer experience. 
- Accounts are a central place for your customer's accounts complete with personal and address information for your record keeping.
- Campaigns are great ways to promote ideas and business goals to partners, customers, and your staff. Start a campaign to better engage with others.

### How to Install

1. (In AlternateCMS) -> click Menu -> System -> Integrations -> (In Integrations Marketplace) Search and Install AlternateCMS Official CRM Plugin
2. Plugin functionality will be available within the Customers Menu
3. Features could potentially be extended further by other plugins and themes.
